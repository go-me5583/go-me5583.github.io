Birds

This site will walk you through a variety of birds.
They come in lots of different ways! What is a bird? Well here is are the main three things for a bird to be a bird-

  1. All birds must lay eggs.
  2. All birds must be warm-blooded
  3. All birds must have feathers, and a bill, and wings(not necessarily for flying).
